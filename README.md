### 2. 📡 Internship at Biznet – Internet Installation & WiFi Troubleshooting

**Description:**
Completed an internship program at **Biznet**, one of Indonesia's leading internet service providers. Directly involved in fiber optic internet installation for customers and handling network issue reports.

**Scope of Work:**

**Internet Installation (Fiber Optic):**
1. Site survey and determining the optimal modem placement at the customer's premises
2. Pulling fiber optic cable from the ODP (Optical Distribution Point) to the customer's home
3. Cable management and tidying at the customer's premises (Home Cable Installation)
4. Configuring and reconfiguring customer modems (SSID, password,etc)
5. Performing ping tests to diagnose connection stability and packet loss

**WiFi Troubleshooting:**
- Identifying and analyzing internet connectivity issues reported by customers
- Resolving problems such as weak signal, disconnections, and slow speeds
- Reconfiguring customer modems
- Coordinating with the technical team for issue escalation
- Documenting problem reports and solutions applied

**Tech & Tools:**
`Fiber Optic Installation` `Modem Configuration` `ODP` `Network Troubleshooting` `Biznet Infrastructure`

**Outcome:**
✅ Successfully installed fiber optic internet at multiple customer locations
✅ Able to independently handle network troubleshooting
✅ Gained real-world work experience in the telecommunications industry
